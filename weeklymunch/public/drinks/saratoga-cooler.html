Dry Saratoga Cooler (NA)

Drinks
2servings

Recipe Credit: Yuru Camp Season 3 anime

Ingredients

  • 2½oz water
  • ½cup sugar (or agave syrup)
  • ½cinnamon stick
  • ¼cup sliced fresh ginger
  • cloves to taste
  • Habanero pepper or red pepper to taste
  • 1lime

Methodtap a step when it's done

  1. Boil the 2.5 oz water and 1/2 cup sugar (or agave syrup)
  2. Once it comes to boil add skin of 1 lime, 1/2 cinnamon stick, 1/4 cup sliced ginger, cloves and pepper to taste
  3. Cut heat after 3 min and let it cool
  4. Add additional sliced ginger for stronger taste
  5. Let it sit overnight
